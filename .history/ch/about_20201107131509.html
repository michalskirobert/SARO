About


Hello,
we are SARO.

The project was created as a result of a desire to help people studying the Polish language. The main language of our site is English but we added languages like Japanese and Korean as well. We hope to add more languages in the future. SARO was created from combining the name “Saki” in Japanese 沙季 and the name Robert. Saki and Robert are Polish-Japanese couple trying to share their knowledge with others.
